Transactions

Learn about IOTA transactions by tracking the journey of coffee from bean to cup





Overview

6 hours
4 lessons

Prepares you to learn:
The Tangle Concepts


Instructors

Lewis is Director of EcoSystems at IOTA. He leads workshops and hosts hackathons and podcasts for the global IOTA community

Nelson designs micro-courses to support training the global IOTA community and he teaches locally in Southern California


Lesson 1: Coffee supply chain use case
Understand transactions required for tracking the journey of coffee from bean to cup

Lesson 2: Supply chain demo
Exercise: practice different types of transactions

Lesson 3: Transactions
Learn how to use the official JavaScript API to send and receive transactions

Lesson 4: Coffee farm water conservation lab
Lab: learn machine-2-machine transactions using the IOTA Community Python API and a Raspberry Pi Zero W plus a soil moisture sensor and a rain sensor

Bonus: Community coffee supply chain projects
IOTA welcomes your contributions. What will you create?
