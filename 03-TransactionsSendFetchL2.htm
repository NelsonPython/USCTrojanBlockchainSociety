IOTA Masked Authenticated Messaging (MAM)

M asked - the message is encrypted
A uthenticated - the device sending the message is confirmed
M essaging - a device may publish a continuous stream of messages to the Tangle

In essence MAM works like a radio where only those with the right frequency can listen in. In MAM only those with the right channel IDs get access to the data. MAM can be used to broadcast three types of messages:
  1. Public messages - public broadcasts
  2. Private messages - broadcasts to members only
  3. Restricted messages - a special type of private message
